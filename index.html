Titanic Info

RMS Titanic was a British built passenger ship which sank in the North Atlantic Ocean in the early morning of the 15th of April 1912. Tragically, the vessel collided with an iceberg during her maiden voyage from Southampton (UK) to New York City (USA). This disatrous occurence resulted in the loss of 1514 souls; attracting world fame and continued interest to the present day. The Titanic was the largest vessel on the ocean when it entered service and retains its place as one of histories deadliest maritime disasters.
